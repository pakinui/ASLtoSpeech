We\textquotesingle{}re working on an app that brings together people who use American Sign Language (ASL) and those who speak verbally. Our Sign Language to Speech App is all about using tech like machine learning and text-\/to-\/speech to turn ASL gestures into spoken words. \item Subtitles\+: Display subtitles in distinct colors for both the ASL user and the person speaking.
