Firstly, Our ASL to Speech App will access the webcam from user\textquotesingle{}s devices to detect the hand object.~\newline
 Once it is detected, It will then be put through the pre-\/trained American Sign Lanauge model to do the language detection.~\newline
 Those detected sing languages will be converted to text and will be shown on the frame as a subtitle. ~\newline
 These sentences made out of Sign Lanauges can be converted to speech by pressing the \textquotesingle{}Translate\textquotesingle{} button. ~\newline
